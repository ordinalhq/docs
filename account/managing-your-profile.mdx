## Overview

Manage your personal Ordinal account settings, preferences, and profile information.

## Profile Information

### Update Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Profile Settings">
    Click your avatar in the top right corner of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Profile Settings">
    Click **Profile Settings** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Your Information">
    Edit your name, avatar, timezone, or language preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** to apply your updates.
    
    <Check>
    Your profile is updated across the entire workspace.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Profile Fields

- **Name** - Your display name visible to team members
- **Email** - Login email (verified)
- **Avatar** - Profile picture shown in comments and activity
- **Timezone** - Your local timezone for scheduling
- **Language** - Interface language

<Tip>
Setting the correct timezone ensures scheduled posts go out at the times you intend.
</Tip>

## Security

### Change Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Security Settings">
    Go to **Profile Settings** → **Security**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Change Password">
    Click the **Change Password** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Passwords">
    Enter your current password, then your new password twice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to update your password.
    
    <Check>
    Your password is updated. You may need to log in again on other devices.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA for additional security:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Security Settings">
    Go to **Profile Settings** → **Security**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Click **Enable 2FA**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR Code">
    Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Verification Code">
    Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Backup Codes">
    Download or copy your backup codes and store them securely.
    
    <Warning>
    Keep backup codes in a safe place. They're the only way to recover your account if you lose access to your authenticator app.
    </Warning>
    
    <Check>
    2FA is now enabled on your account.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't change email">
    Email changes require verification. Contact support if you need to change your login email.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lost 2FA device">
    Use one of your backup codes to log in, then disable and re-enable 2FA with a new device.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Session showing unknown location">
    VPNs and mobile networks can show unexpected locations. If you don't recognize a session, revoke it immediately and change your password.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
