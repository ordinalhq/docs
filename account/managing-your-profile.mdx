Personalize your Ordinal experience with your profile image, display name, and calendar preferences. These settings apply to your account across all workspaces you belong to.

![User Preferences](/images/user-preferences.jpg)

## Accessing your settings

1. Click **Settings** in the left sidebar
2. Select **Preferences** under the Account Settings section.

You'll see your personal account settings - separate from workspace-level settings like timezone or billing.

## Profile image

Your profile image appears throughout Ordinal - in comments, real-time collaboration indicators, team member lists, and approval requests. It helps teammates identify you at a glance.

**To upload or change your image:**

1. Go to [**Settings → Preferences**](https://app.tryordinal.com/settings/user)
2. Click **Choose File** under Profile Image
3. Select an image (JPG or PNG)
4. Crop and position your image in the modal
5. Click **Save Profile Image**

**To remove your image:**

Hover over your current profile image and click the **X** button that appears.

<Tip>
  Recommended size is 300x300 pixels or larger. Images are automatically cropped to a square.
</Tip>

## Name

Your name is displayed in comments, activity feeds, approval requests, and team member lists.

**To update your name:**

1. Go to [**Settings → Preferences**](https://app.tryordinal.com/settings/user)
2. Edit the **First Name** and **Last Name** fields
3. Click **Save**

<Note>
  Your name is shared across all workspaces you belong to. Changing it here updates it everywhere.
</Note>

## Calendar start of week

Choose which day your calendar week starts on. This affects how the calendar grid is displayed.

**To change your start of week:**

1. Go to [**Settings → Preferences**](https://app.tryordinal.com/settings/user)
2. Find **Calendar Start of Week**
3. Select **Sunday** or **Monday**

Your preference saves automatically and the calendar updates immediately.

## Email and password

Your email address is used for signing in and receiving notifications. Currently, email changes require contacting support at [**support@tryordinal.com**](mailto:support@tryordinal.com).

If you prefer to setup a password-based login, or need to reset your password, logout first and visit the [login page](https://app.tryordinal.com/login). From there, click **Continue with Email / Password** and then **Forgot Password**. You'll receive an email with a link to setup a new password.
