## Overview

Ordinal offers flexible plans to fit teams of all sizes. Choose the plan that works best for your needs.

## Plans

### Free

Get started with Ordinal at no cost:
- 3 social profiles
- 30 scheduled posts per month
- Basic analytics
- 1 team member

### Pro

For growing teams and creators:
- 10 social profiles
- Unlimited scheduled posts
- Advanced analytics
- 5 team members
- Priority support

### Business

For teams that need more:
- 25 social profiles
- Unlimited scheduled posts
- Custom analytics & reports
- Unlimited team members
- Custom roles & permissions
- Dedicated support

### Enterprise

Custom solutions for large organizations:
- Unlimited social profiles
- Custom integrations
- SSO/SAML
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantees
- Custom contracts

## Upgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Subscription Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade">
    Click the **Upgrade** button to view available plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Plan">
    Choose your new plan from the options.
    
    <Tip>
    Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly billing.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Payment">
    Enter your payment information if not already saved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review and confirm your upgrade.
    
    <Check>
    Your new features are available immediately after upgrade.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downgrading

<Warning>
Downgrading may result in loss of access to features and connected profiles that exceed your new plan limits.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Subscription Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Change Plan">
    Click **Change Plan** to view options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New Plan">
    Choose the plan you want to downgrade to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Changes">
    Review what features and limits will change.
    
    <Warning>
    If you have more profiles or team members than the new plan allows, you'll need to remove some before the downgrade takes effect.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Downgrade">
    Confirm your downgrade. Changes take effect at the end of your current billing period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  <Accordion title="Features missing after downgrade">
    Some features are only available on higher plans. Upgrade to regain access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Need Enterprise features">
    Contact our sales team for custom Enterprise pricing and features.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
