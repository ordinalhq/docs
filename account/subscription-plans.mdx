## Overview

Ordinal offers flexible plans to fit teams of all sizes. Choose the plan that works best for your needs.

## Plans

### Free

Get started with Ordinal at no cost:
- 3 social profiles
- 30 scheduled posts per month
- Basic analytics
- 1 team member

### Pro

For growing teams and creators:
- 10 social profiles
- Unlimited scheduled posts
- Advanced analytics
- 5 team members
- Priority support

### Business

For teams that need more:
- 25 social profiles
- Unlimited scheduled posts
- Custom analytics & reports
- Unlimited team members
- Custom roles & permissions
- Dedicated support

### Enterprise

Custom solutions for large organizations:
- Unlimited social profiles
- Custom integrations
- SSO/SAML
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantees
- Custom contracts

## Upgrading

1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Select your new plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm upgrade

## Downgrading

<Warning>
Downgrading may result in loss of access to features and connected profiles that exceed your new plan limits.
</Warning>

1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Review what changes
5. Confirm downgrade
