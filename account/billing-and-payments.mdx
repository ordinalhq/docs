## Overview

Manage your Ordinal subscription billing, payment methods, and invoices.

## Payment Methods

### Add Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter card details
4. Save payment method

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- ACH bank transfer (Business+)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise)

### Update Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click on your current payment method
3. Update details or add new method
4. Set as default if needed

## Billing Cycle

### Monthly Billing

- Billed on the same date each month
- Based on your signup date
- Cancel anytime

### Annual Billing

- Pay for 12 months upfront
- Save 20% compared to monthly
- Billed once per year

### Changing Billing Cycle

Switch between monthly and annual:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Change Billing Cycle**
3. Select monthly or annual
4. Confirm change

## Invoices

### View Invoices

Access your billing history:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Invoices**
3. View or download any invoice

### Invoice Details

Each invoice includes:
- Invoice number and date
- Billing period
- Itemized charges
- Payment status

### Billing Email

Set where invoices are sent:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Update **Billing Email**
3. Add additional recipients if needed

## Cancellation

### Cancel Subscription

1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Complete cancellation survey
4. Confirm cancellation

<Note>
You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
</Note>

### After Cancellation

- Access continues until period ends
- Data retained for 30 days
- Can reactivate anytime
- Export data before deletion
