## Overview

Manage your Ordinal subscription billing, payment methods, and invoices.

## Payment Methods

### Add Payment Method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Payment Method">
    Click the **Add Payment Method** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Card Details">
    Enter your card number, expiration date, and CVV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to add the payment method.
    
    <Check>
    Your payment method is saved and ready to use.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- ACH bank transfer (Business+)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise)

### Update Payment Method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Payment Method">
    Click on your current payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update or Replace">
    Update the details or add a new payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Default">
    If adding a new method, set it as default if desired.
    
    <Check>
    Your payment method is updated for future charges.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Billing Cycle

### Monthly Billing

- Billed on the same date each month
- Based on your signup date
- Cancel anytime

### Annual Billing

- Pay for 12 months upfront
- Save 20% compared to monthly
- Billed once per year

<Tip>
Switch to annual billing to save 20% on your subscription.
</Tip>

### Changing Billing Cycle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Subscription Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Change Billing Cycle">
    Select the **Change Billing Cycle** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New Cycle">
    Choose monthly or annual billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review the prorated charges and confirm.
    
    <Check>
    Your billing cycle is updated. Any prorated amount is applied to your next invoice.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invoices

### View Invoices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invoices">
    Select **Invoices** to view your billing history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View or Download">
    Click any invoice to view or download as PDF.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Invoice Details

Each invoice includes:
- Invoice number and date
- Billing period
- Itemized charges
- Payment status

### Billing Email

Set where invoices are sent:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Billing Email">
    Enter or update the **Billing Email** address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Recipients">
    Optionally add additional email recipients for invoices.
    
    <Check>
    Future invoices will be sent to the specified email addresses.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancellation

### Cancel Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Subscription Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Cancel Subscription">
    Select **Cancel Subscription**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Survey">
    Tell us why you're leaving (optional but helpful).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Confirm that you want to cancel.
    
    <Note>
    You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### After Cancellation

- Access continues until period ends
- Data retained for 30 days
- Can reactivate anytime
- Export data before deletion

<Warning>
Export any data you need before your retention period ends. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment failed">
    Check that your card details are correct and the card isn't expired. Try a different payment method if the issue persists.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Unexpected charge">
    Check your invoice history for details. Charges may include prorated amounts when upgrading mid-cycle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Need a refund">
    Contact support within 14 days of a charge for refund requests.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
