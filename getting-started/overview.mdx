## What is Ordinal?

Ordinal is a comprehensive social media management platform that helps you create, schedule, and analyze your content across multiple platforms. Whether you're a solo creator or a large team, Ordinal streamlines your social media workflow.

## Supported Platforms

Ordinal integrates with all major social platforms:

- [**LinkedIn**](/social-profiles/linkedin)
- [**Twitter / X**](/social-profiles/twitter)
- [**Instagram**](/social-profiles/instagram)
- [**Facebook**](/social-profiles/facebook)
- [**TikTok**](/social-profiles/tiktok)
- [**YouTube Shorts**](/social-profiles/youtube-shorts)
- [**Threads**](/social-profiles/threads)
- [**Webflow**](/social-profiles/webflow)
- [**Slack**](/social-profiles/slack)
- [**Discord**](/social-profiles/discord)

<Tip>
  You can connect multiple accounts from each platform - perfect for managing multiple brands, clients, or regional accounts.
</Tip>

## Who Uses Ordinal?

- **Marketing teams** managing brand social presence
- **Agencies** handling multiple client accounts
- **Creators** building personal brands
- **Businesses** coordinating company communications
