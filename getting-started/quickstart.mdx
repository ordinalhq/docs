Get started with Ordinal in just three steps. By the end of this guide, you'll have connected your first social profile and created your first post.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up for an Ordinal account at [ordinal.com](https://ordinal.com).
    
    <Check>
    You should receive a confirmation email and be redirected to your new dashboard.
    </Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your Social Profiles">
    Navigate to the Social Profiles section and connect your first account.
    
    1. Click **Add Profile** in the sidebar
    2. Select your platform (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, etc.)
    3. Authorize Ordinal to access your account in the popup window
    
    <Check>
    Your connected profile appears in the Social Profiles list with a green "Connected" status.
    </Check>
    
    <Tip>
    Start with the platform where you post most frequently. You can always add more profiles later.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Post">
    Now you're ready to create and schedule your first post.
    
    1. Go to **Posts** â†’ **Drafting Posts**
    2. Write your content in the composer
    3. Select which profiles to post to
    4. Click **Schedule** to set a time, or **Publish Now** for immediate posting
    
    <Check>
    Your post appears in the Calendar view at its scheduled time, or shows as "Published" if you posted immediately.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
