## Core Concepts

Understanding these key concepts will help you get the most out of Ordinal.

### Social Profiles

A social profile is a connected account from any supported platform (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, etc.). You can connect multiple profiles and manage them all from Ordinal.

<Tip>
Connect all your social accounts to manage everything from one place. Each profile appears in your sidebar for quick access.
</Tip>

### Posts

Posts are the content you create and publish to your social profiles. A single post can be published to multiple profiles simultaneously, with automatic adaptation to each platform's requirements.

### Campaigns

Campaigns are collections of related posts organized around a theme, product launch, or marketing initiative. They help you:
- Plan coordinated content efforts
- Track aggregate performance
- Keep related posts organized

### Labels

Labels are tags you can apply to posts for organization and filtering. Use them to categorize content by:
- Topic (industry news, product updates, tips)
- Content type (educational, promotional)
- Priority (urgent, evergreen)

### Statuses

Statuses track where a post is in your workflow:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Draft** | Work in progress |
| **Pending Approval** | Awaiting review |
| **Scheduled** | Approved and queued for publishing |
| **Published** | Live on your social profiles |
| **Failed** | Publishing failed (requires attention) |

### Approvals

The approval workflow lets team members review and approve posts before they're published. This ensures quality and brand consistency across all your content.

## The Content Workflow

A typical post flows through Ordinal like this:

1. **Create** - Draft your content in the composer
2. **Review** - Submit for approval (if enabled)
3. **Schedule** - Set when to publish
4. **Publish** - Content goes live automatically
5. **Analyze** - Track performance in analytics

## Platform-Specific Features

Different platforms have unique features and requirements. Ordinal handles these automatically while giving you platform-specific controls when needed.

For example:
- **Twitter** - Thread composer with character counts
- **Instagram** - Carousel builder with visual preview
- **LinkedIn** - Document post support
- **TikTok** - Video scheduling
