## Overview

Connect your Twitter/X accounts to Ordinal to schedule tweets, threads, and engage with your audience effectively.

## Connecting Twitter / X

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Profile">
    Go to **Social Profiles** â†’ **Add Profile** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Twitter / X">
    Choose **Twitter / X** from the list of available platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Log in to Twitter and authorize Ordinal in the popup window.
    
    <Check>
    Your Twitter account appears in the Social Profiles list with a green "Connected" status.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Content Types

- **Tweets** - Standard text posts (up to 280 characters, or 25,000 for Premium)
- **Threads** - Multi-tweet stories
- **Images** - Up to 4 images per tweet
- **GIFs** - Animated content
- **Videos** - Up to 2:20 in length
- **Polls** - Engage your audience

## Features

### Thread Composer

Create engaging Twitter threads with our thread composer:
- Write multiple tweets in sequence
- Preview how your thread will appear
- Schedule the entire thread at once

<Tip>
Use threads to break down complex topics. Each tweet in a thread should be able to stand alone while contributing to the overall narrative.
</Tip>

### Character Counter

Real-time character counting helps you stay within limits while maximizing your message.

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Optimal posting frequency">
    3-5 tweets per day is ideal for maintaining visibility without overwhelming followers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Engagement timing">
    Peak engagement times are typically 8-10 AM and 6-9 PM in your audience's timezone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Hashtag strategy">
    Use 1-2 relevant hashtags per tweet for optimal reach.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Tweet failed to post">
    Check if your tweet exceeds character limits or contains content that violates Twitter's policies. Also verify your account isn't rate-limited.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Images not uploading">
    Ensure images are under 5MB and in JPG, PNG, GIF, or WEBP format.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Thread posts out of order">
    If threads post incorrectly, try reducing the number of tweets or increasing the delay between posts in settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
