## Overview

Connect your Instagram business or creator accounts to Ordinal to schedule posts, stories, and reels.

## Connecting Instagram

<Note>
Instagram requires a Facebook Business Page connection. Make sure your Instagram account is converted to a Business or Creator account before proceeding.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Profile">
    Go to **Social Profiles** → **Add Profile** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Instagram">
    Choose **Instagram** from the list of available platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect via Facebook">
    Log in to your Facebook account that's linked to your Instagram Business profile.
    
    <Tip>
    If you manage multiple Instagram accounts, make sure you're logged into the correct Facebook account.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Instagram Account">
    Choose the Instagram account you want to connect from the list of available accounts.
    
    <Check>
    Your Instagram profile appears in the Social Profiles list with a green "Connected" status.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Content Types

- **Feed posts** - Single images or carousels
- **Reels** - Short-form video content
- **Stories** - Ephemeral 24-hour content
- **Carousels** - Up to 10 images/videos

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't find my Instagram account">
    Ensure your Instagram is converted to a Business or Creator account and is linked to a Facebook Page. You can check this in Instagram Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Connection failed or expired">
    Try disconnecting and reconnecting your Facebook account. Make sure you grant all requested permissions during authorization.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Posts failing to publish">
    Check that your content meets Instagram's requirements: images should be between 320x320 and 1080x1350 pixels, and videos should be under 60 seconds for feed posts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
