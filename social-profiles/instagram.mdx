## Overview

Connect your Instagram business or creator accounts to Ordinal to schedule posts, stories, and reels.

## Connecting Instagram

<Note>
Instagram requires a Facebook Business Page connection. Make sure your Instagram account is converted to a Business or Creator account.
</Note>

1. Go to **Social Profiles** â†’ **Add Profile**
2. Select **Instagram**
3. Connect through your linked Facebook account
4. Select the Instagram account to connect
5. Your Instagram account is now connected

## Supported Content Types

- **Feed posts** - Single images or carousels
- **Reels** - Short-form video content
- **Stories** - Ephemeral 24-hour content
- **Carousels** - Up to 10 images/videos

## Features

### Visual Preview

See exactly how your post will appear on Instagram before publishing.

### Carousel Builder

Create engaging carousels with our drag-and-drop builder:
- Reorder slides easily
- Preview swipe experience
- Add individual captions per slide
