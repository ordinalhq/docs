## Overview

Connect your TikTok business account to Ordinal to schedule videos and track your content performance.

## Connecting TikTok

<Note>
TikTok requires a Business account for API access. Personal accounts cannot be connected.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Profile">
    Go to **Social Profiles** → **Add Profile** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select TikTok">
    Choose **TikTok** from the list of available platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In to TikTok">
    Enter your TikTok business account credentials in the popup window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Review and accept the permissions Ordinal needs to post on your behalf.
    
    <Check>
    Your TikTok account appears in the Social Profiles list with a green "Connected" status.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Content Types

- **Videos** - Standard TikTok videos
- **Photo slideshows** - Multiple images as a video

## Features

### Video Scheduling

Upload and schedule your TikTok videos in advance. Set your preferred posting times and let Ordinal handle the rest.

<Tip>
Prepare your videos in 9:16 vertical format for best results. TikTok favors native vertical content.
</Tip>

### Caption & Hashtags

Craft your captions and add hashtags within Ordinal before scheduling.

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Optimal posting times">
    TikTok engagement peaks during evening hours (7 PM - 11 PM) and weekends.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Hashtag strategy">
    Mix trending hashtags with niche-specific ones. Use 3-5 hashtags per post.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Content consistency">
    Post regularly (1-3 times daily) to build momentum with the algorithm.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Limitations

<Warning>
Be aware of these TikTok-specific limitations when scheduling content.
</Warning>

- Videos must be prepared before uploading (no in-app editing)
- Some features like duets and stitches are only available in the native app

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't connect my account">
    Ensure your TikTok account is a Business account. You can switch to a Business account in TikTok Settings → Manage Account → Switch to Business Account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Video upload failed">
    Check that your video meets TikTok's requirements: 9:16 aspect ratio, under 10 minutes, and under 287.6 MB.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Post not appearing">
    TikTok may take a few minutes to process and publish videos. If the post still doesn't appear after 30 minutes, try re-uploading.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
