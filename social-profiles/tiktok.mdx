## Connecting TikTok

Go to [**Settings → Profiles → Scheduling → TikTok**](https://app.tryordinal.com/settings/profiles/scheduling/tiktok), click "Connect TikTok", and sign in. Authorize Ordinal and your account will appear in your connected profiles.

## Drafting a TikTok Post

### What you can post

| Content                                  | Supported | Notes                              |
| ---------------------------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------- |
| [Videos](#video-requirements)            | ✅         | Single video per post              |
| [Photo carousels](#photo-requirements)   | ✅         | 1–35 images per post               |
| Trending sounds                          | ❌         | API limitation                     |
| Filters/effects                          | ❌         | API limitation                     |
| Duets/Stitches                           | Video only | Not available on photo posts      |
| Text overlays                            | ❌         | API limitation                     |

Use the **Video / Photo** toggle at the top of the editor to switch between content types. Switching clears the current attachments, so pick the type that matches your media before uploading.

### Caption & Formatting

Write captions to accompany your video:

- **Bold / Italic**: Use the toolbar buttons for Unicode formatting
- **Hashtags**: Type `#` followed by your tag - they'll link to TikTok's tag page when published
- **@Mentions**: Type `@` followed by a username to tag other creators

Captions can be up to **2,200 characters**.

<Tip>
  Mix trending hashtags with niche-specific ones. 3–5 per post is a good balance for discoverability without looking spammy.
</Tip>

### Post Preview

As you draft your post, the editor shows a live preview that matches how your content will appear in the TikTok feed - complete with your profile picture, username, and caption overlay. Photo posts show a carousel preview you can swipe through to check image order before publishing.

### Video Requirements

For video posts, here are the specs:

| Spec         | Limit                              |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| Formats      | MP4, MOV                           |
| Resolution   | 360px to 4096px (width and height) |
| Aspect ratio | 9:16 (vertical) recommended        |
| Duration     | 3 seconds to 10 minutes            |
| File size    | 350mb maximum                      |

Vertical video (9:16) performs best and fills the entire screen. Horizontal or square videos work but won't have the same impact. Files over 125MB are automatically compressed before upload.

### Photo Requirements

Photo posts publish as a swipeable carousel. You can upload up to 35 images, and Ordinal automatically resizes anything wider than 1080px to keep uploads fast.

| Spec        | Limit                |
| ----------- | -------------------- |
| Formats     | JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP |
| Images      | 1 to 35 per post     |
| Max width   | 1080px (auto-resized) |
| File size   | 10mb per image       |

After uploading, drag images to reorder them - the first image is used as the cover. You cannot mix images and videos in the same post.

#### Title and description

Photo posts use a **title** and **description** instead of a single caption:

- **Title**: Up to 90 characters. Shown above the carousel.
- **Description**: Up to 4,000 characters. Supports hashtags and `@mentions` just like video captions.

## Post Settings

Configure how viewers can interact with your post. You'll find these options below the editor:

- **Privacy**: Choose who can see your post - Public, Followers, Friends, or just yourself
- **Comments**: Enable or disable comments
- **Duets** (video only): Allow others to create Duets with your video
- **Stitch** (video only): Allow others to Stitch your video into their content
- **Auto-add music** (photo only): Let TikTok automatically add a soundtrack to your photo carousel
- **Sponsored**: Mark the post as promotional content
- **Paid Partnership**: Disclose paid brand partnerships (only appears if Sponsored is enabled)

<Tip>
  Enabling Duets and Stitches on video posts can increase your reach - other creators using your content exposes you to their audience.
</Tip>
