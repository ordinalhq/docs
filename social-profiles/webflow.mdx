## Overview

Connect your Webflow site to Ordinal to publish blog posts and content directly to your website's CMS.

## Connecting Webflow

1. Go to **Social Profiles** â†’ **Add Profile**
2. Select **Webflow**
3. Authorize Ordinal to access your Webflow account
4. Select the site and collection to connect
5. Your Webflow site is now connected

## Supported Features

- **Blog posts** - Publish to your CMS collections
- **Content updates** - Update existing items
- **Draft management** - Save as draft before publishing

## Use Cases

### Synchronized Content

Keep your social media and blog content synchronized:
- Draft a blog post in Ordinal
- Schedule social posts to promote it
- Publish everything at once

### Content Repurposing

Turn your social content into blog posts:
- Expand tweet threads into articles
- Convert LinkedIn posts to blog content

## Setup Requirements

<Note>
Webflow CMS is required. Sites on the free plan without CMS cannot be connected.
</Note>

Your Webflow site needs:
- An active CMS plan
- At least one collection for blog posts
- API access enabled
