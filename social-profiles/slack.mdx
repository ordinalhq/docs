## Overview

Connect Slack to Ordinal to schedule messages to your team channels. Perfect for recurring announcements, reminders, and coordinated communications.

## Connecting Slack

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Profile">
    Go to **Social Profiles** â†’ **Add Profile** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Slack">
    Choose **Slack** from the list of available platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Workspace">
    Select the Slack workspace you want to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Channels">
    Choose which channels Ordinal can post to.
    
    <Tip>
    Start with a dedicated announcements channel. You can add more channels later.
    </Tip>
    
    <Check>
    Your Slack workspace appears in the Social Profiles list with a green "Connected" status.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use Cases

### Team Announcements

Schedule important announcements to go out at optimal times:
- Product launch notifications
- Weekly updates
- Meeting reminders

### Content Coordination

Notify your team when content goes live:
- Alert when social posts are published
- Share content for engagement
- Coordinate amplification efforts

### Automated Updates

Set up recurring messages:
- Daily standup reminders
- Weekly metric summaries
- Monthly newsletter reminders

## Features

### Channel Selection

Choose specific channels for different types of messages. Keep your communications organized and relevant.

### Message Formatting

Use Slack's rich formatting:
- Bold, italic, and code formatting
- Links and mentions
- Emoji and reactions

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Timing considerations">
    Schedule messages during work hours when team members are active. Avoid early morning or late night messages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Channel etiquette">
    Use dedicated channels for automated messages to avoid cluttering active discussion channels.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Channel not appearing">
    Ensure the Ordinal bot has been invited to the channel. Type `/invite @Ordinal` in the channel.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Messages not posting">
    Check that your Slack workspace admin hasn't restricted bot access. Contact your Slack admin if needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
