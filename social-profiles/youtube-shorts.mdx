## Overview

Connect your YouTube channel to Ordinal to schedule YouTube Shorts and manage your short-form video content.

## Connecting YouTube

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Profile">
    Go to **Social Profiles** â†’ **Add Profile** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select YouTube">
    Choose **YouTube** from the list of available platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign In with Google">
    Log in to the Google account associated with your YouTube channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Channel">
    If you manage multiple channels, select the one you want to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant Permissions">
    Accept the permissions Ordinal needs to upload and manage videos.
    
    <Check>
    Your YouTube channel appears in the Social Profiles list with a green "Connected" status.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Content Types

- **YouTube Shorts** - Vertical videos up to 60 seconds
- **Standard videos** - Regular YouTube uploads

## Features

### Shorts Scheduling

Schedule your YouTube Shorts in advance:
- Upload vertical videos (9:16 aspect ratio)
- Add titles and descriptions
- Set visibility and scheduling time

<Tip>
Include "#Shorts" in your title or description to ensure YouTube categorizes your video correctly.
</Tip>

### Cross-Platform Repurposing

Easily repurpose content from TikTok and Instagram Reels to YouTube Shorts.

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Video format">
    Shorts should be vertical (9:16), under 60 seconds, and have engaging first 3 seconds.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Titles and descriptions">
    Use searchable titles with relevant keywords. Shorts are discoverable through search.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Posting frequency">
    Consistent daily posting helps build momentum on the Shorts shelf.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Limitations

<Warning>
Be aware of these YouTube-specific limitations when scheduling content.
</Warning>

- Shorts must be under 60 seconds
- Some YouTube features require the native YouTube Studio

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Channel not appearing">
    Ensure you're logged into the correct Google account. If you manage multiple accounts, try signing out and back in.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Video not showing as Short">
    Make sure your video is vertical (9:16), under 60 seconds, and includes "#Shorts" in the title or description.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Upload processing slowly">
    YouTube can take time to process videos. Higher resolution videos take longer. Check YouTube Studio for processing status.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
