## Connecting a Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Social Profiles">
    Click **Social Profiles** in the left sidebar of your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a New Profile">
    Click the **Add Profile** button in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Platform">
    Choose the platform you want to connect from the list of available options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Follow the authorization prompts in the popup window. You may need to log in to your social account.
    
    <Check>
    Your profile appears in the Social Profiles list with a green "Connected" status.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Multiple Profiles

You can connect multiple accounts from the same platform. This is useful for managing:

- Multiple brands or clients
- Personal and professional accounts
- Regional or language-specific accounts
