## Overview

Connect your Threads account to Ordinal to schedule text-based posts and engage in conversations.

## Connecting Threads

<Note>
Threads is connected through your Instagram account. Make sure your Instagram is already connected to Ordinal.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Profile">
    Go to **Social Profiles** â†’ **Add Profile** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Threads">
    Choose **Threads** from the list of available platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize via Instagram">
    Select your connected Instagram account to authorize Threads access.
    
    <Check>
    Your Threads profile appears in the Social Profiles list with a green "Connected" status.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Content Types

- **Text posts** - Up to 500 characters
- **Images** - Single or multiple images
- **Videos** - Short-form video content
- **Links** - Share URLs with previews

## Features

### Cross-Posting

Easily cross-post between Threads and other text-based platforms like Twitter/X.

<Tip>
While you can cross-post, consider tailoring your content slightly for each platform's audience and norms.
</Tip>

### Thread Creation

Create multi-post threads to share longer stories or insights.

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Content style">
    Threads favors conversational, authentic content. Be genuine and engage in discussions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Engagement">
    Reply to comments and engage with other users to build your presence.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cross-posting">
    Consider tailoring content for Threads rather than copying exactly from Twitter.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't find Threads option">
    Ensure your Instagram account is connected first. Threads requires an active Instagram connection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Posts not appearing">
    Check that your Threads account is active and not restricted. Try posting directly in the Threads app to verify.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
