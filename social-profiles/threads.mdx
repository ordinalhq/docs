## Overview

Connect your Threads account to Ordinal to schedule text-based posts and engage in conversations.

## Connecting Threads

<Note>
Threads is connected through your Instagram account. Make sure your Instagram is already connected to Ordinal.
</Note>

1. Go to **Social Profiles** â†’ **Add Profile**
2. Select **Threads**
3. Authorize through your connected Instagram account
4. Your Threads account is now connected

## Supported Content Types

- **Text posts** - Up to 500 characters
- **Images** - Single or multiple images
- **Videos** - Short-form video content
- **Links** - Share URLs with previews

## Features

### Cross-Posting

Easily cross-post between Threads and other text-based platforms like Twitter/X.

### Thread Creation

Create multi-post threads to share longer stories or insights.
