## Overview

Connect your LinkedIn personal profiles and company pages to Ordinal to schedule posts, track engagement, and grow your professional presence.

## Connecting LinkedIn

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Profile">
    Go to **Social Profiles** â†’ **Add Profile** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select LinkedIn">
    Choose **LinkedIn** from the list of available platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Account Type">
    Select whether you want to connect a personal profile or a company page.
    
    <Tip>
    Company pages offer additional features like article publishing and follower analytics.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Log in to LinkedIn and authorize Ordinal in the popup window.
    
    <Check>
    Your LinkedIn profile appears in the Social Profiles list with a green "Connected" status.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Content Types

- **Text posts** - Share updates and insights
- **Images** - Single or multiple images
- **Documents** - PDF carousels and presentations
- **Articles** - Long-form content (company pages)
- **Polls** - Engage your audience with questions

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Optimal posting times">
    LinkedIn engagement peaks during business hours, particularly Tuesday through Thursday, 8-10 AM and 12 PM in your audience's timezone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Content recommendations">
    Mix professional insights, industry news, and personal stories for better engagement.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Hashtag usage">
    Use 3-5 relevant hashtags to increase discoverability without appearing spammy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Limitations

<Warning>
Be aware of these LinkedIn-specific limitations when scheduling content.
</Warning>

- LinkedIn API limits the number of posts per day
- Some features may only be available for company pages
- Video scheduling requires LinkedIn's native scheduling

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't connect my company page">
    You must be an admin of the LinkedIn company page to connect it. Check your page admin settings in LinkedIn.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Authorization expired">
    LinkedIn tokens expire periodically. Disconnect and reconnect your profile in Social Profiles settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Document posts not working">
    Ensure your PDF is under 100MB and has fewer than 300 pages. LinkedIn may reject larger files.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
