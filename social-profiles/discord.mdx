## Overview

Connect Discord to Ordinal to schedule messages to your community servers. Keep your community engaged with timely announcements and updates.

## Connecting Discord

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Profile">
    Go to **Social Profiles** â†’ **Add Profile** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Discord">
    Choose **Discord** from the list of available platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Log in to Discord and authorize Ordinal to access your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Server and Channels">
    Choose the server and specific channels where you want to schedule messages.
    
    <Tip>
    Select your announcements or updates channel to keep scheduled content organized.
    </Tip>
    
    <Check>
    Your Discord server appears in the Social Profiles list with a green "Connected" status.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Channel organization">
    Use dedicated channels for scheduled content (e.g., #announcements) to keep discussions separate from automated messages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Embed formatting">
    Use rich embeds for important announcements to make them stand out from regular messages.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Bot not appearing in server">
    Ensure you have "Manage Server" permissions to add the Ordinal bot. Ask a server admin if needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't post to channel">
    Check channel permissions. The Ordinal bot needs "Send Messages" and "Embed Links" permissions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Embeds not displaying">
    Verify "Embed Links" permission is enabled for the Ordinal bot in channel settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
