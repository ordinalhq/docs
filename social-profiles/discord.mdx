## Overview

Connect Discord to Ordinal to schedule messages to your community servers. Keep your community engaged with timely announcements and updates.

2. Select **Discord**

## Use Cases

### Community Announcements

Schedule announcements for your community:
- Event notifications
- Product updates
- Community highlights

### Content Sharing

Share your social content with your Discord community:
- New blog posts
- Social media highlights
- Behind-the-scenes content

### Engagement Campaigns

Coordinate community activities:
- AMA announcements
- Giveaway launches
- Feedback requests

## Features

### Server & Channel Selection

Connect multiple servers and select specific channels for different content types.

### Rich Embeds

Create visually appealing messages with Discord embeds:
- Custom titles and descriptions
- Images and thumbnails
- Colored sidebars
- Footer information
