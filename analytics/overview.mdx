## Overview

Analytics gives you insights into your social media performance. Track engagement, growth, and content effectiveness across all connected profiles.

## Dashboard

The analytics dashboard shows key metrics at a glance:

- **Total Reach** - How many people saw your content
- **Engagement** - Likes, comments, shares, and clicks
- **Follower Growth** - Net new followers over time
- **Top Posts** - Your best performing content

<Tip>
Check your dashboard weekly to spot trends and adjust your content strategy.
</Tip>

## Profile Analytics

### Individual Profile Metrics

View detailed analytics for each profile:
- Follower count and growth
- Post performance
- Engagement rate
- Best posting times

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Profile">
    Click on a profile in the analytics sidebar to view its metrics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Date Range">
    Select the time period you want to analyze.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Metrics">
    Browse through engagement, reach, and growth data.
    
    <Check>
    Metrics update in real-time as your content collects engagement.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Cross-Profile Comparison

Compare performance across profiles:
- Side-by-side metrics
- Identify top performers
- Spot trends and patterns

## Content Analytics

### Post Performance

Track how individual posts perform:
- Impressions and reach
- Engagement breakdown
- Click-through rate
- Audience reactions

### Content Type Analysis

See which content types work best:
- Images vs. videos vs. text
- Carousels vs. single images
- Short-form vs. long-form

<Tip>
Use content type analysis to double down on what's working and experiment with underperforming formats.
</Tip>

## Reporting

### Automated Reports

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Report">
    Go to **Analytics** → **Reports** → **New Report**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Metrics">
    Choose which metrics to include in the report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Schedule">
    Choose delivery frequency: daily, weekly, or monthly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Recipients">
    Enter email addresses for report delivery.
    
    <Check>
    Reports are automatically generated and emailed on schedule.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Custom Reports

Build reports with the metrics you need:
- Select date ranges
- Choose specific profiles
- Pick metrics to include
- Add custom branding

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Metrics not updating">
    Social platforms provide data with a delay (typically 24-48 hours). Recent posts may not show complete data yet.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Discrepancies with platform data">
    Minor differences are normal due to different counting methods and timing. Large discrepancies may indicate a sync issue—try reconnecting the profile.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reports not being delivered">
    Check spam folders. Verify recipient email addresses are correct and add Ordinal to your email contacts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
