## Overview

Analytics gives you insights into your social media performance. Track engagement, growth, and content effectiveness across all connected profiles.

## Dashboard

The analytics dashboard shows key metrics at a glance:

- **Total Reach** - How many people saw your content
- **Engagement** - Likes, comments, shares, and clicks
- **Follower Growth** - Net new followers over time
- **Top Posts** - Your best performing content

## Profile Analytics

### Individual Profile Metrics

View detailed analytics for each profile:
- Follower count and growth
- Post performance
- Engagement rate
- Best posting times

### Cross-Profile Comparison

Compare performance across profiles:
- Side-by-side metrics
- Identify top performers
- Spot trends and patterns

## Content Analytics

### Post Performance

Track how individual posts perform:
- Impressions and reach
- Engagement breakdown
- Click-through rate
- Audience reactions

### Content Type Analysis

See which content types work best:
- Images vs. videos vs. text
- Carousels vs. single images
- Short-form vs. long-form

## Reporting

### Automated Reports

Schedule regular reports:
- Daily, weekly, or monthly
- Email delivery
- PDF or CSV format

### Custom Reports

Build reports with the metrics you need:
- Select date ranges
- Choose specific profiles
- Pick metrics to include
- Add custom branding

## Data Export

Export your data for external analysis:
- CSV download
- API access
- Integration with BI tools
