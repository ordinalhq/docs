## Overview

Shortlinks make your URLs cleaner and trackable. Create branded short links for any URL and track clicks, conversions, and engagement.

## Creating Shortlinks

### Quick Create

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Shortlinks">
    Click **New Shortlink** in the Shortlinks section, or paste a URL anywhere in Ordinal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Destination URL">
    Paste the full URL you want to shorten.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the Short Code">
    Optionally create a memorable custom slug (e.g., `/summer-sale`).
    
    <Tip>
    Use descriptive slugs that hint at the content—they're more memorable and trustworthy.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Link">
    Click **Create** to generate your shortlink.
    
    <Check>
    Your shortlink is created and ready to use. Click to copy it to your clipboard.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### In Post Composer

Create shortlinks while drafting:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste a URL">
    Paste any URL into your post content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Shorten">
    Click the **Shorten** button that appears on the URL.
    
    <Check>
    The URL is replaced with a tracked shortlink automatically.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Shortlink Features

### Custom Slugs

Create memorable short links:
- `ord.co/product-launch`
- `ord.co/summer-sale`
- `ord.co/webinar-signup`

### Custom Domains

Use your own domain for complete brand control:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Custom Domain">
    Go to **Settings** → **Shortlinks** → **Custom Domains**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Domain">
    Enter the domain you want to use (e.g., `links.yourbrand.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure DNS">
    Add the required DNS records to your domain registrar.
    
    <Check>
    Once verified, all new shortlinks use your custom domain.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### UTM Parameters

Automatically add tracking parameters:
- Campaign source
- Medium
- Campaign name
- Custom parameters

<Tip>
Set up UTM templates for consistent tracking across all your links.
</Tip>

## Managing Shortlinks

### Edit Links

Update shortlink settings:
- Change destination URL (link stays the same)
- Update UTM parameters
- Modify expiration

### Archive Links

Remove unused links from active view:
- Links still work
- Hidden from main list
- Accessible in archive

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Custom slug already taken">
    Choose a different slug, or add a number or date to make it unique.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Click counts not updating">
    Analytics may take a few minutes to update. If still not updating after an hour, check that the link is correctly configured.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Custom domain not verifying">
    Double-check DNS records are correctly configured. DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
