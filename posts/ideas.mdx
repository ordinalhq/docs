## Overview

The Ideas feature is your content brainstorming space. Capture ideas as they come, organize them, and turn them into polished posts when you're ready.

## Capturing Ideas

### Quick Capture

Add ideas from anywhere in Ordinal:
- Press `Cmd/Ctrl + I` to open quick capture
- Type your idea and save
- Add tags for organization

### Browser Extension

Capture ideas while browsing:
- Save articles for inspiration
- Clip quotes and images
- Add notes and context

### Mobile App

Capture ideas on the go:
- Voice-to-text capture
- Photo inspiration
- Quick notes

## Organizing Ideas

### Categories

Organize ideas by category:
- Content type (educational, promotional, etc.)
- Platform focus
- Campaign or project
- Priority level

### Tags

Add tags for flexible organization:
- Topic tags
- Status tags
- Audience tags

### Boards

Create visual boards:
- Kanban-style organization
- Drag and drop between columns
- Collaborate with team members

## From Idea to Post

Turn ideas into content:

1. **Select an idea** from your collection
2. **Click "Create Post"** to start drafting
3. **Expand and refine** your idea
4. **Schedule or publish** when ready

## Collaboration

### Shared Ideas

Share ideas with your team:
- Invite team members to collaborate
- Comment on ideas
- Vote on favorites

### Idea Assignments

Assign ideas to team members:
- Clear ownership
- Due dates
- Progress tracking
