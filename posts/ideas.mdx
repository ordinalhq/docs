## Overview

The Ideas feature is your content brainstorming space. Capture ideas as they come, organize them, and turn them into polished posts when you're ready.

## Capturing Ideas

### Quick Capture

Add ideas from anywhere in Ordinal:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Quick Capture">
    Press `Cmd/Ctrl + I` to open the quick capture dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type Your Idea">
    Write your ideaâ€”it can be a headline, a rough draft, or just a topic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Tags">
    Optionally add tags for organization.
    
    <Check>
    Your idea is saved and appears in the Ideas list.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Browser Extension

Capture ideas while browsing:
- Save articles for inspiration
- Clip quotes and images
- Add notes and context

<Tip>
Install the Ordinal browser extension to capture ideas from any webpage with a single click.
</Tip>

### Mobile App

Capture ideas on the go:
- Voice-to-text capture
- Photo inspiration
- Quick notes

## Organizing Ideas

### Categories

Organize ideas by category:
- Content type (educational, promotional, etc.)
- Platform focus
- Campaign or project
- Priority level

### Tags

Add tags for flexible organization:
- Topic tags
- Status tags
- Audience tags

### Boards

Create visual boards:
- Kanban-style organization
- Drag and drop between columns
- Collaborate with team members

## From Idea to Post

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an Idea">
    Open the idea you want to develop from your Ideas collection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Post">
    Click the **Create Post** button to open the composer with your idea pre-filled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Expand and Refine">
    Develop your idea into a complete post with media and platform selection.
    
    <Check>
    The original idea is marked as "Converted" and linked to the new post.
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule or Publish">
    Schedule or publish your post when ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Quick capture not working">
    Ensure you're focused on the Ordinal app window. The keyboard shortcut only works within Ordinal.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't find old ideas">
    Check your filters and search. Ideas may be filtered by category, tag, or status.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Browser extension not saving">
    Verify you're logged into the extension with the same account. Try signing out and back in.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
