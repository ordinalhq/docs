Post Review is an AI assistant that reads your draft and returns a short list of suggested improvements - things like typos, clarity issues, weak hooks, missing calls-to-action, or platform-specific concerns. It runs automatically when you open it, so you can catch issues without breaking flow.

## When to use it

Use Post Review when you want a quick second pass before scheduling or publishing:

- Right before you set a publish time, from the schedule modal
- Anytime while editing, from the post header
- On longer posts where small fixes have the biggest impact

Post Review never edits your post. It only surfaces suggestions - you decide what to apply.

## Running a review

You can run a review from two places:

| Location           | How to open                                                       |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Schedule modal     | Click **Schedule Post**. The review section appears in the modal. |
| Post header        | Click **Post Review** in the post header.                         |

In both places, the review runs automatically the first time you open it. When it finishes, you'll see either **No suggestions** or a count like **3 Suggestions Found**.

Expand the section to view each suggestion. Click **Re-run** to generate a fresh review after you make edits.

## Suggestions

Each suggestion includes:

- A short description of the issue
- The relevant snippet from your post (when applicable)
- A recommended change you can apply or ignore

Suggestions are advisory. Apply the ones that fit your voice and skip the rest.
