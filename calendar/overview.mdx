## Overview

The Calendar gives you a visual overview of all your scheduled content across all connected profiles. Plan your content strategy and ensure consistent posting.

## Calendar Views

### Month View

See your entire month at a glance:
- View all scheduled posts
- Identify gaps in your content schedule
- Plan around holidays and events

<Tip>
Use Month view to spot scheduling gaps. Aim for consistent posting frequency across the month.
</Tip>

### Week View

Get a detailed look at your weekly schedule:
- Hour-by-hour breakdown
- Easy drag-and-drop rescheduling
- Quick access to post details

### Day View

Focus on a single day:
- Detailed view of all scheduled content
- Fine-tune posting times
- Last-minute adjustments

## Features

### Drag and Drop

Easily reschedule posts by dragging them to a new date or time. The calendar updates in real-time.

<Check>
A confirmation toast appears when you successfully reschedule a post.
</Check>

### Color Coding

Posts are color-coded by:
- Platform (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)
- Status (Draft, Scheduled, Published)
- Campaign or Label

### Filters

Filter your calendar view by:
- Social profile
- Content type
- Campaign
- Team member

## Quick Actions

From the calendar, you can:
- **Click** a post to view details
- **Drag** to reschedule
- **Right-click** for quick actions (edit, duplicate, delete)
- **Double-click** an empty slot to create a new post

## Creating Posts from Calendar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find an Empty Slot">
    Navigate to the date and time when you want to post.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Double-Click">
    Double-click the empty slot to open the post composer.
    
    <Check>
    The composer opens with the date and time pre-filled.
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Post">
    Write your content, add media, and select profiles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Schedule** to confirm the pre-filled time, or adjust as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Posts not appearing on calendar">
    Check your filters. You may have filtered to a specific profile or status that excludes some posts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't drag posts">
    Ensure you're clicking directly on the post card. Published posts cannot be rescheduled.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Wrong timezone showing">
    Check your account timezone in Settings â†’ Profile. The calendar displays in your account timezone.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
