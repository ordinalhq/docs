## Overview

The Calendar gives you a visual overview of all your scheduled content across all connected profiles. Plan your content strategy and ensure consistent posting.

## Calendar Views

### Month View

See your entire month at a glance:
- View all scheduled posts
- Identify gaps in your content schedule
- Plan around holidays and events

### Week View

Get a detailed look at your weekly schedule:
- Hour-by-hour breakdown
- Easy drag-and-drop rescheduling
- Quick access to post details

### Day View

Focus on a single day:
- Detailed view of all scheduled content
- Fine-tune posting times
- Last-minute adjustments

## Features

### Drag and Drop

Easily reschedule posts by dragging them to a new date or time. The calendar updates in real-time.

### Color Coding

Posts are color-coded by:
- Platform (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)
- Status (Draft, Scheduled, Published)
- Campaign or Label

### Filters

Filter your calendar view by:
- Social profile
- Content type
- Campaign
- Team member

## Quick Actions

From the calendar, you can:
- **Click** a post to view details
- **Drag** to reschedule
- **Right-click** for quick actions (edit, duplicate, delete)
- **Double-click** an empty slot to create a new post
