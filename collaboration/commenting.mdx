## Overview

Comments enable team collaboration directly on posts. Leave feedback, ask questions, and discuss content without leaving Ordinal.

## Adding Comments

### On Posts

Comment on any post:
1. Open the post
2. Click in the comment area
3. Type your comment
4. Press Enter or click Send

### Inline Comments

Highlight specific text to comment:
1. Select text in the post
2. Click the comment icon
3. Add your feedback
4. Comment is linked to that text

## Comment Features

### Mentions

Tag team members in comments:
- Type `@` followed by their name
- They receive a notification
- Creates a direct conversation thread

## Managing Comments

### Reply Threads

Keep conversations organized:
- Reply directly to comments
- Threaded discussions stay together
- Easy to follow conversations

### Resolve Comments

Mark comments as resolved:
- Indicates feedback was addressed
- Keeps comment for reference
- Reduces clutter

### Delete Comments

Remove comments when needed:
- Only your own comments
- Admins can delete any comment

## Notifications

Stay updated on comment activity:
- New comments on your posts
- Replies to your comments
- Mentions in any comment
