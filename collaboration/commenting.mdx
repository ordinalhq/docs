## Overview

Comments enable team collaboration directly on posts. Leave feedback, ask questions, and discuss content without leaving Ordinal.

## Adding Comments

### On Posts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Post">
    Click on any post to open the detail view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Comment Area">
    Scroll to the comments section below the post preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type Your Comment">
    Write your feedback in the comment field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Press **Enter** or click **Send** to post your comment.
    
    <Check>
    Your comment appears in the thread and the post author is notified.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Inline Comments

Highlight specific text to leave targeted feedback:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Text">
    Highlight the specific text you want to comment on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Comment Icon">
    A comment icon appears‚Äîclick it to open the inline comment dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your Feedback">
    Write your comment about the selected text.
    
    <Check>
    The comment links directly to that text, making it easy to understand the context.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Inline comments are perfect for suggesting specific word changes or asking about particular phrases.
</Tip>

## Managing Comments

### Reply Threads

Keep conversations organized:
- Reply directly to comments
- Threaded discussions stay together
- Easy to follow conversations

### Resolve Comments

Mark comments as resolved when feedback has been addressed:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Address the Feedback">
    Make the requested changes to the post.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Resolve">
    Click the **Resolve** button on the comment.
    
    <Check>
    The comment is marked as resolved and collapses, reducing visual clutter.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Delete Comments

Remove comments when needed:
- Only your own comments
- Admins can delete any comment

## Notifications

Stay updated on comment activity:
- New comments on your posts
- Replies to your comments
- Mentions in any comment

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Comment not saving">
    Check your internet connection. Comments require an active connection to save.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't mention a team member">
    Ensure the person is a member of your workspace. Only active workspace members can be mentioned.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Notifications not working">
    Check notification settings for the user being mentioned. They may have disabled comment notifications.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
