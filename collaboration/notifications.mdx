## Overview

Stay informed about important activity without being overwhelmed. Configure notifications to match your workflow and preferences.

## Notification Types

### Content Notifications

- Post approved or rejected
- Post published successfully
- Post failed to publish
- Scheduled post reminder

### Collaboration Notifications

- New comment on your post
- Reply to your comment
- Mentioned in a comment
- Approval request

### Team Notifications

- New team member joined
- Permissions changed
- Profile connected/disconnected

## Notification Channels

### Email

Receive notifications via email:
- **Immediate** - As events happen
- **Daily digest** - Summary once per day
- **Weekly digest** - Weekly summary

### Slack

Get notified in Slack:
- Personal DMs
- Team channels
- Thread replies

### In-App

Dashboard notifications:
- Notification bell icon
- Real-time updates
- Click to navigate

### Mobile Push

Push notifications on mobile:
- Urgent notifications only
- All notifications
- Scheduled quiet hours

## Configuring Notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Notifications** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Notification Type">
    Choose which type of notification you want to configure (content, collaboration, or team).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Channels">
    Select which channels should receive this notification type (email, Slack, in-app, mobile).
    
    <Tip>
    Use immediate notifications for urgent items like failed posts, and digests for routine updates.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Frequency">
    Choose how often you receive notifications—immediate, daily digest, or weekly digest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Preferences">
    Click **Save** to apply your settings.
    
    <Check>
    Your notification preferences are updated immediately.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notification Settings

### Quiet Hours

Set times when you won't receive notifications:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Quiet Hours">
    Toggle on Quiet Hours in notification settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Schedule">
    Define start time, end time, and which days of the week.
    
    <Tip>
    Set quiet hours for evenings and weekends to maintain work-life balance.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Exceptions">
    Optionally allow urgent notifications (like failed posts) to bypass quiet hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Notification Grouping

Reduce notification volume:
- Group similar notifications
- Batch by time period
- Summary instead of individual

## Managing Notifications

### Mark as Read

Clear notification badges:
- Click individual notifications
- "Mark all as read" option

### Notification History

View past notifications:
- Access from notification panel
- Searchable history
- Click to navigate to source

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Not receiving email notifications">
    Check your spam folder. Add Ordinal's email domain to your contacts to ensure delivery.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Slack notifications not working">
    Verify the Slack integration is connected in Settings → Integrations. Re-authorize if needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Too many notifications">
    Switch from immediate to digest notifications, or enable notification grouping to reduce volume.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
