## Overview

Stay informed about important activity without being overwhelmed. Configure notifications to match your workflow and preferences.

## Notification Types

### Content Notifications

- Post approved or rejected
- Post published successfully
- Post failed to publish
- Scheduled post reminder

### Collaboration Notifications

- New comment on your post
- Reply to your comment
- Mentioned in a comment
- Approval request

### Team Notifications

- New team member joined
- Permissions changed
- Profile connected/disconnected

## Notification Channels

### Email

Receive notifications via email:
- **Immediate** - As events happen
- **Daily digest** - Summary once per day
- **Weekly digest** - Weekly summary

### Slack

Get notified in Slack:
- Personal DMs
- Team channels
- Thread replies

### In-App

Dashboard notifications:
- Notification bell icon
- Real-time updates
- Click to navigate

### Mobile Push

Push notifications on mobile:
- Urgent notifications only
- All notifications
- Scheduled quiet hours

## Notification Settings

### Quiet Hours

Set times when you won't receive notifications:
- Define start and end times
- Select days of week
- Exception for urgent items

### Notification Grouping

Reduce notification volume:
- Group similar notifications
- Batch by time period
- Summary instead of individual

## Managing Notifications

### Mark as Read

Clear notification badges:
- Click individual notifications
- "Mark all as read" option

### Notification History

View past notifications:
- Access from notification panel
- Searchable history
- Click to navigate to source
