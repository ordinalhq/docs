## Overview

Campaigns help you organize related posts around a theme, product launch, event, or marketing initiative. Track all your coordinated content efforts in one place.

## Creating a Campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Campaigns">
    Go to **Campaigns** in the left sidebar and click **New Campaign**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Campaign">
    Enter a descriptive name and optional description for your campaign.
    
    <Tip>
    Use clear, descriptive names like "Q1 Product Launch" or "Summer Sale 2024" for easy identification.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Campaign Dates">
    Optionally set start and end dates to define your campaign timeline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Posts">
    Add existing drafts or create new posts directly within the campaign.
    
    <Check>
    Your campaign appears in the Campaigns list with a post count and status overview.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Campaign Features

### Campaign Dashboard

Each campaign has its own dashboard showing:
- All posts in the campaign
- Publishing status
- Aggregate performance metrics
- Team activity

### Post Organization

Add posts to campaigns:
- During post creation
- From existing drafts
- Bulk add multiple posts

<Tip>
You can add a post to a campaign at any timeâ€”during creation, from the Posts list, or from the post detail view.
</Tip>

### Timeline View

See your campaign unfold over time:
- Visual timeline of all posts
- Status indicators
- Gap identification

## Campaign Metrics

Track campaign performance:
- **Total reach** - Combined audience across all posts
- **Engagement** - Likes, comments, shares
- **Click-through** - Link clicks and conversions
- **Best performers** - Top posts in the campaign

<Check>
Campaign metrics update in real-time as your posts publish and collect engagement.
</Check>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Post not showing in campaign">
    Check that the post is properly assigned to the campaign. Open the post and verify the campaign field is set correctly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Metrics not updating">
    Campaign metrics refresh periodically. For real-time data, click the refresh button on the campaign dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't delete campaign">
    Campaigns with published posts cannot be deleted. Remove all posts from the campaign first, or archive the campaign instead.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
