## Overview

Campaigns help you organize related posts around a theme, product launch, event, or marketing initiative. Track all your coordinated content efforts in one place.

## Creating a Campaign

1. Go to **Campaigns** â†’ **New Campaign**
2. Enter campaign name and description
3. Set campaign dates (optional)
4. Add posts to the campaign
5. Track progress and performance

## Campaign Features

### Campaign Dashboard

Each campaign has its own dashboard showing:
- All posts in the campaign
- Publishing status
- Aggregate performance metrics
- Team activity

### Post Organization

Add posts to campaigns:
- During post creation
- From existing drafts
- Bulk add multiple posts

### Timeline View

See your campaign unfold over time:
- Visual timeline of all posts
- Status indicators
- Gap identification

## Campaign Metrics

Track campaign performance:
- **Total reach** - Combined audience across all posts
- **Engagement** - Likes, comments, shares
- **Click-through** - Link clicks and conversions
- **Best performers** - Top posts in the campaign
