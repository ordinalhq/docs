## Overview

Integrate Slack with Ordinal to receive notifications, coordinate approvals, and keep your team in sync with your social media activity.

## Connecting Slack

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Slack">
    Locate **Slack** in the integrations list and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Workspace">
    Choose your Slack workspace from the authorization screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Channels">
    Select which channels Ordinal can post to.
    
    <Tip>
    Start with a dedicated channel like #social-media to keep notifications organized.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Click **Authorize** to complete the connection.
    
    <Check>
    Slack shows as "Connected" in your integrations list.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Features

### Notifications

Receive Ordinal notifications in Slack:
- Post published notifications
- Approval requests
- Comment mentions
- Failed post alerts

### Approval Workflows

Handle approvals directly in Slack:
- Receive approval requests as messages
- Approve or reject with buttons
- Add feedback in thread

<Tip>
Slack approvals let your team review content without logging into Ordinal.
</Tip>

### Marketing Boost Channels

Notify your team when content needs engagement:
- Send boost requests to dedicated channels
- One-click links to engage with posts
- Track participation

## Configuration

### Channel Mapping

Set up channels for different notification types:

| Notification Type | Suggested Channel |
|------------------|-------------------|
| All notifications | #social-media |
| Approvals | #content-approvals |
| Alerts | #social-alerts |
| Boosts | #team-boosts |

### Notification Preferences

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Slack Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** → **Slack**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Notification Types">
    Choose which notifications to send to Slack.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map Channels">
    Assign each notification type to a specific channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to apply your preferences.
    
    <Check>
    Notifications will now appear in your configured Slack channels.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Slack Commands

Use slash commands in Slack:
- `/ordinal status` - View scheduled posts
- `/ordinal approve` - See pending approvals
- `/ordinal analytics` - Quick metrics summary

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Not receiving notifications">
    Check that the correct channel is selected and Ordinal has permission to post. Verify the channel is public or Ordinal is invited to private channels.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Approval buttons not working">
    Make sure you're still logged into Ordinal. Button actions require an active session.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Need to reconnect">
    If workspace settings changed, disconnect Slack in Ordinal and reconnect to refresh permissions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
